The moving finger

"The moving finger" is a detective story written by Agatha Christie. Agatha Christie was a famous writer in the 20th century.

Jerry Burton - a pilot who got into an accident and now is an invalid.
Joanna Burton - Mr. Burtons younger sister, said to be attractive. She likes dancing, cocktails and love affairs.
Megan Symmington - very clumsy, young girl not too pretty. Daughter of Mrs Symmington and her precious husband. Not very liked by the villagers.

Mr Burton and his sister move to Lymstock so that he can compleatly heal after the accident. After a week they get an anonymous letter saying horrible things. And so these letters are coming to every person who lives in Lymstock till one is driven to despair and the first death has come. But it doesn't stop there. To find out who is the killer, read the story yourself.

I recommend this book to those who like detective stories and mystery. I don't recommend this book to small children because it may be a bit hard to follow. I personally liked this book. It wasvery interesting, you could think along the storyline and make your own assumptions.

The mist

The Mist is a horror and psychological novella.

In Bridgton, Maine a soldier wakes up in a forest with a memory loss, he loses his dog in the mist and sees that it has been torn apart by it,
he runs to the police station but no one belives him. Suddenly the whole city is covered in mist, it kills people and tears them apart if you are in it longer than ~3 minutes.
A lady discovers that she is immune to it by having a bet with a priest where she got cover in a church (priest was killed).
Mia, Kevin, Adrian and Bryan (later Jonah) get together to find Kevins daughter and wife who are stuck in the mall.
They go from a police station to a house, to a hospital, then to church, other places, finally reaching the mall and uniting with his family.

The body in the library

I read “The body in the library”, it’s a detective and mystery story written by Agatha Christie.

One morning at Gossington Hall the maid wakes up the house owners named Arthur Bantry and Dolly Bantry to inform them about the
terrifying news – a dead body has been found in their library. Dolly Bantry knows that there’s only one person to call, old Miss Marple. While the
investigators are trying to identify the dead girl, they get a call from the hotel in the next city. A hotel guest named Conway Jefferson reports a girl missing, the girls cousin,
an employee at the hotel, Josie Turner identifies the dead girl as her cousin Ruby Keene. As the investigation goes on turns out that Conway Jefferson wanted to adopt
Ruby and was already getting the papers ready. Conway Jefferson was already old and not so healthy anymore, after his death he wanted to leave all
his money to Ruby. A few days later another dead young girl was found.

Will the investigators find a way to connect the deaths? Will they figure out who’s the murderer before they kill another person?
